Cut, Copy, & Paste Organisms

Organisms can be cut or copied from one simulation and pasted into another. Also you can delete an organism (Del key).

  • Copy Organism:
    Select an organism by left clicking on it. Ctrl-C will copy the organism to the clip board. The simulation is not altered.

  • Cut Organism:
    Select an organism by left clicking on it. Ctrl-X will cut the organism from the universe (it will be removed), and the organism will be placed in the clip board.

  • Paste Organism:
    If you have cut or copied an organism, then you can press Ctrl-V to paste the organism into the current simulation. The organism is pasted somehwere near the center of your simulation.

  • Delete Organism:
    Select an organism by left clicking on it. Pressing Del will delete the organism from the universe.

Why do this?

If you find a clever organism and wish to experiment on it in seperate universe, you can use the copy/paste features to do so. Also you might want to see how successful a creature from one simulation is, when transplanted into another simulation.

(you will also find the Tweak Energy and Move Organism features useful for this kind of experimentation.)
